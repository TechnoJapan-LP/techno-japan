Techno Japan is a cultural media platform focused on Japan's underground techno and house music scene. We go beyond event listings — introducing artists, venues, festivals, and club culture with editorial perspective and scene credibility.

We exist to make Japan's underground more visible and more meaningful, to audiences both inside Japan and across the world.
